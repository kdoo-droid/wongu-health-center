We Can Help

Whatever You're Going Through, You Don't Have to Face It Alone

Our patients come to us with all kinds of health challenges. Some have tried everything else. Some are just getting started. Whatever brought you here, we'll listen first and build a plan that's right for you.

Stroke Recovery

Rebuilding strength and mobility after a stroke. We're here to support your recovery journey, one session at a time.

Cancer Support

Going through chemo or radiation is tough enough. We help manage the nausea, fatigue, and pain so you can focus on healing.

Mental Health

Anxiety, depression, PTSD, insomnia — you don't have to white-knuckle through it. We offer gentle, effective support, including specialized veteran care.

Chronic Illness

Living with an autoimmune condition, fibromyalgia, or chronic fatigue? We'll work with you to manage symptoms and improve your quality of life.

Pain Management

Back pain, migraines, sciatica, arthritis — if you've been living with pain, we can help you find real relief without relying on medications.

Pregnancy & Birth

From fertility support to postpartum recovery, we're with you through every stage. Ask about our pregnancy care packages.

Weight Management

A holistic approach to healthy weight — combining acupuncture, herbal medicine, and practical lifestyle guidance. No crash diets, just real support.

Women's Health

Painful periods, menopause, hormonal imbalance, endometriosis — we understand, and we have effective, natural treatments that can help.

Ask Dr. Yu or Dr. Sekine about package treatment plans for pregnancy, weight management, fertility, and more.

Why Patients Choose Us

Real Care From People Who Actually Know Your Name

We're not a revolving-door clinic. When you come to Wongu, your practitioner takes the time to understand your full health picture — not just the symptom that brought you in. As the teaching clinic of Wongu University, our interns spend 1.5 to 2 hours on your first visit because thorough care takes time. And every single session is supervised by a licensed OMD who reviews your diagnosis and approves your treatment plan.

The result? You get unhurried, attentive care at prices that won't break the bank — starting at $80 for your first visit. That's the Wongu difference.
