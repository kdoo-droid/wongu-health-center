The MD-OMD Unified Medical Clinic

Wongu Health Center operates Southern Nevada's first structured integrated care model. Patients continue receiving physician-led diagnosis and treatment, while our licensed Doctors of Oriental Medicine provide evidence-informed adjunct therapies to improve recovery outcomes, symptom management, and treatment tolerance.

The Collaborative is not an alternative system â€” it's a coordinated extension of existing healthcare, supporting physicians, improving patient outcomes, and expanding access for underserved populations. The program includes structured referral pathways, shared documentation, and outcomes tracking.

As the only accredited institution of its kind in Nevada, with an established clinic, existing patient base, and owned facilities, Wongu offers both the infrastructure and clinical foundation to deliver this integrated model of care.
