Our Acupuncture & Oriental Medicine Services

From acupuncture and cupping to herbal medicine and wellness consultations, Wongu Health Center offers personalized care designed to support healing, restore balance, and help you feel your best. Treatments are provided by supervised graduate interns or licensed OMDs.

Core Service

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is the foundation of care at Wongu Health Center. Using sterile, hair-thin needles placed at specific points, our practitioners help regulate circulation, calm the nervous system, ease pain, and support the body's natural healing response.

Each treatment is personalized to your symptoms, health history, and goals. Depending on your needs, your care plan may also include lifestyle guidance, herbal recommendations, or other supportive therapies.

Common reasons patients seek acupuncture:

✓ Chronic and acute pain ✓ Migraines and headaches ✓ Sciatica and back pain ✓ Neck and shoulder tension ✓ Arthritis and joint pain ✓ Neuropathy ✓ Anxiety and depression ✓ Insomnia ✓ Digestive concerns ✓ Allergies and sinus issues

Therapeutic Support

Cupping Therapy

Cupping therapy uses glass cups to create gentle suction on the skin, helping release muscular tension, improve circulation, and support recovery. At Wongu Health Center, we offer traditional fire cupping sessions lasting up to 30 minutes.

Cupping can be a helpful option for patients experiencing tightness, soreness, stress, or lingering pain patterns. OMD cupping visits include consultation with a licensed Doctor of Oriental Medicine to determine the best approach for your condition.

Benefits may include:

✓ Pain and inflammation relief ✓ Improved circulation ✓ Muscle recovery ✓ Deep relaxation ✓ Respiratory support ✓ Reduced muscular tension

Traditional Formulas

Chinese Herbal Medicine

Chinese herbal medicine is an important part of Oriental medicine and allows us to support healing between visits. After identifying your pattern of imbalance, our practitioners select a formula tailored to your symptoms, constitution, and treatment goals.

Formulas may be prepared as teas, pills, powders, tinctures, or topical preparations. Our herbal dispensary carries a wide range of classical formulas and individual herbs, with prescriptions available starting at $10 per day.

Herbal consultations may support:

✓ Digestive balance ✓ Immune support ✓ Stress and sleep support ✓ Women's health concerns ✓ Pain and recovery ✓ Long-term wellness plans
Book Herbal Consultation
Personalized Guidance

Wellness Consultations

Wellness consultations are ideal for patients who want a clearer understanding of their health from an Oriental medicine perspective. These visits focus on identifying patterns of imbalance and building a plan that supports your symptoms, lifestyle, and long-term goals.

During your consultation, our practitioners may review your health history, current concerns, tongue and pulse findings, stress levels, digestion, sleep, and daily habits that can influence healing. You will leave with practical next steps and a treatment strategy tailored to your needs.

Consultations may include:

✓ Tongue & pulse assessment ✓ Lifestyle guidance ✓ Symptom pattern review ✓ Nutrition recommendations ✓ Treatment planning ✓ Herbal or acupuncture referrals
Request a Wellness Consultation
MD-OMD Unified Medical Clinic

Integrated Care for Complex Conditions

When patients need more coordinated support, our MD-OMD Unified Medical Clinic brings physician oversight together with evidence-informed Oriental medicine to complement ongoing medical care.

Stroke Recovery Support

Coordinated post-stroke care designed to support mobility, spasticity reduction, pain relief, and day-to-day recovery goals alongside your medical team.

Oncology Supportive Care

Adjunct support for patients navigating chemotherapy, radiation, or recovery, with care focused on nausea, fatigue, sleep, stress, and overall comfort.

Mental Health & Veteran PTSD

Compassionate support for anxiety, depression, PTSD, insomnia, and stress-related symptoms, including care for veterans. VA insurance accepted.

Chronic Illness Management

Long-term integrative care for autoimmune conditions, chronic pain, digestive disorders, and other complex concerns that benefit from steady, coordinated treatment.

Focused Treatment Plans

Specialized Treatment Packages

Dr. Yu and Dr. Sekine offer personalized multi-visit packages for patients who want more structured care around specific health goals.

Pregnancy & Birth

Supportive care for fertility, prenatal wellness, morning sickness, labor preparation, and postpartum recovery with a plan tailored to each stage.

Weight Management

A structured program that may combine acupuncture, herbal medicine, nutrition guidance, and lifestyle support to help patients pursue sustainable weight goals.
