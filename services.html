Our Acupuncture & Oriental Medicine Services

Oriental medicine has been used for thousands of years to prevent, diagnose, and treat diseases. All treatments are performed by 3rd and 4th-year graduate students under the supervision of highly trained faculty, or by licensed OMDs.

Core Service

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is the insertion of very thin needles into specific points on the body to stimulate the flow of qi (energy) and blood that nourish and strengthen the body and rebalance bodily disharmonies. Acupuncture is the primary treatment at Wongu Health Center, and other modalities may be added depending on the diagnosis.

Treatments may also include herbal prescriptions, diet recommendations, and recommendations on tai ji or qi gong exercises.
