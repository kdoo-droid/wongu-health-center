Our Acupuncture & Oriental Medicine Services

From acupuncture and cupping to custom herbal formulas, herbal teas, and wellness consultations, Wongu Health Center offers personalized care designed to support healing, restore balance, and help you feel your best. Treatments are provided by supervised graduate interns or licensed OMDs.

Core Service

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is the foundation of care at Wongu Health Center. Using sterile, hair-thin needles placed at specific points, our practitioners help regulate circulation, calm the nervous system, ease pain, and support the body's natural healing response.

Each treatment is personalized to your symptoms, health history, and goals. Depending on your needs, your care plan may also include lifestyle guidance, herbal recommendations, or other supportive therapies.

Common reasons patients seek acupuncture:

✓ Chronic and acute pain ✓ Migraines and headaches ✓ Sciatica and back pain ✓ Neck and shoulder tension ✓ Arthritis and joint pain ✓ Neuropathy ✓ Anxiety and depression ✓ Insomnia ✓ Digestive concerns ✓ Allergies and sinus issues

Therapeutic Support

Cupping Therapy

Cupping therapy uses glass cups to create gentle suction on the skin, helping release muscular tension, improve circulation, and support recovery. At Wongu Health Center, we offer traditional fire cupping sessions lasting up to 30 minutes.

Cupping can be a helpful option for patients experiencing tightness, soreness, stress, or lingering pain patterns. OMD cupping visits include consultation with a licensed OMD to determine the best approach for your condition.

Benefits may include:

✓ Pain and inflammation relief ✓ Improved circulation ✓ Muscle recovery ✓ Deep relaxation ✓ Respiratory support ✓ Reduced muscular tension

Custom Formulas & Teas

Chinese Herbal Medicine

Chinese herbal medicine is an important part of Oriental medicine and helps support care between visits. After identifying your pattern of imbalance, our practitioners may recommend a formula or tea tailored to your symptoms, constitution, and treatment goals.

Our herbal dispensary carries classical formulas and individual herbs that may be prepared as teas, pills, powders, tinctures, or topical preparations. Patients can purchase recommended formulas through the clinic, with herbal prescriptions available starting at $10 per day.

If you already receive acupuncture at Wongu, ask your practitioner whether a custom formula or herbal tea could support your care plan.

Herbal consultations may include:

✓ Custom herbal formulas ✓ Herbal teas and decoctions ✓ Pills, powders, tinctures, and topicals ✓ Digestive balance ✓ Stress and sleep support ✓ Refill and follow-up guidance
Ask About Herbal Formulas
Personalized Guidance

Wellness Consultations

Wellness consultations are ideal for patients who want a clearer understanding of their health from an Oriental medicine perspective. These visits focus on identifying patterns of imbalance and building a plan that supports your symptoms, lifestyle, and long-term goals.

During your consultation, our practitioners may review your health history, current concerns, tongue and pulse findings, stress levels, digestion, sleep, and daily habits that can influence healing. You will leave with practical next steps and, when appropriate, herbal formula or tea recommendations tailored to your needs.

Consultations may include:

✓ Tongue & pulse assessment ✓ Lifestyle guidance ✓ Symptom pattern review ✓ Nutrition recommendations ✓ Treatment planning ✓ Herbal formula or acupuncture recommendations

Common Care Goals
