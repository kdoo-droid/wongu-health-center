What to Expect at the Student Clinic

Book Your Appointment Online

Select "Student Clinic" when booking through our Unified Practice portal. Complete your intake forms digitally before arriving â€” no paper forms or medical data on our website.

Meet Your Intern & Supervisor

Your intern will conduct a thorough health intake, including traditional diagnostic methods. Their supervising OMD will review the findings and approve the treatment plan.

Receive Your Treatment

Your intern performs the acupuncture (and any additional modalities) under direct OMD oversight. Initial intern visits typically run 1.5 to 2 hours, while follow-up sessions are about 1 hour â€” giving your intern time to be thorough and attentive.

Follow-Up & Plan

After your session, your intern and supervisor will discuss next steps, answer questions, and recommend a treatment frequency tailored to your needs and goals.

Safety & Quality

Is the Student Clinic Safe?

Absolutely. Here's why you can feel confident choosing our teaching clinic.

Direct OMD Supervision

A licensed Oriental Medicine Doctor is physically present and directly supervises every treatment. They review all diagnoses and approve all treatment plans before any needles are placed.

Rigorous Academic Training

Our interns have completed hundreds of hours of coursework in anatomy, acupuncture theory, Chinese herbal medicine, and clinical safety before ever treating patients.

Sterile, Modern Facility

Our clinic meets all Nevada health department standards. We use single-use, sterile, disposable needles, and follow strict sanitation and infection control protocols.

Patient-Centered Approach

Interns are trained to be thorough, communicative, and gentle. Because they're learning, they often take more time and care than practitioners in busy private practices.
